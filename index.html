CAFM Integrations  ·  Integration Catalogue facilio.

Integrate your stack.

From ERP and BMS to client intake and asset data, Facilio connects the systems that keep FM operations moving. Four core integration categories — and the customer proof behind connected FM execution.

Where Facilio Connects
ERP, Finance & Billing
Where Operations, Finance, and Billing Stay in Sync.
Every completed work order flows directly to your ERP — triggering purchase requests, reconciling costs against contracts, and closing the loop on invoicing. Finance gets real-time visibility the moment work is done, not when someone remembers to send a report.
Client Intake & Service
Every Service Request. One System of Record.
Whether clients raise requests via WhatsApp, email, or a dedicated portal — each one enters Facilio as a structured work order with SLA timers running from first contact. No manual rekeying. No request falls through. A full audit trail from first message to sign-off.

Email Client Portal Mobile App
BMS, BAS & Building Systems
Turn Building Signals Into Actionable Workflows.
Facilio connects to your existing BMS, meters, and third-party controllers via open protocols. When a fault is detected or a threshold is crossed, a work order is raised and assigned automatically — cutting response time and reducing reactive call volume across your portfolio.
BIM, Legacy CAFM & Asset Data
One Source of Truth. Across Every BIM Model.
Link BIM models directly to asset records in Facilio — so maintenance schedules, inspection histories, and space data remain tied to the model during migration and beyond. Asset history, service records, and digital twin data stay aligned, regardless of what legacy CAFM you're moving from.
